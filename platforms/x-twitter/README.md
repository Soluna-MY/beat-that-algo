# 𝕏 (Twitter) Algorithm Decoded

The 𝕏 algorithm has been open-sourced, giving us a rare look under the hood. It is primarily built to optimize for **Recency, Relevance, and Engagement Velocity**.

## 🚀 The Weighted Ranking System (Top Signals)
𝕏 uses a scoring system to determine what appears in the **"For You"** tab:
1. **Bookmarks (30x Boost):** Currently the strongest signal. If a user saves your post, the algorithm treats it as "Highly Valuable."
2. **Retweets (20x Boost):** Amplifies reach to the retweeter's followers.
3. **Likes (0.5x Boost):** Important for initial momentum but weaker than shares or saves.
4. **Replies (1x Boost):** Conversational value is key, but "Ratioing" (more replies than likes) can lead to a post being demoted.
5. **Premium Boost:** Subscribers (Blue Checkmarks) receive a significant visibility multiplier in both the feed and reply sections.

---

## 🧵 The "Thread" Architecture
Threads are the best way to bypass the "short-form" limit and dominate the feed.
- **The Hook (Post 1):** Must promise a specific outcome (e.g., "I spent 100 hours researching X so you don't have to. Here are 5 life-changing takeaways:").
- **The Mid-Thread CTA:** On post 3 or 4, ask for a Retweet or Follow. This captures users who are already invested in the content.
- **The Conclusion:** Always end with a "TL;DR" (Too Long; Didn't Read) summary for skimmers.

## 🎞️ Video & Multimedia
𝕏 is pivoting to a "Video-First" platform.
- **Native Video:** Uploading directly to 𝕏 (up to 2 hours for Premium) is currently favored over any other media type.
- **Vertical Format:** Record in 9:16. The mobile feed is now optimized for vertical scrolling similar to TikTok.

## 🔍 Avoiding the "Shadow-Demotion"
𝕏 is aggressive about limiting reach for certain behaviors:
- **External Links:** Posts with links to other social networks or websites are heavily suppressed. **Workaround:** Post the link in the 2nd or 3rd post of a thread, or add it to the main post *after* it has gained initial traction.
- **Keyword Suppression:** Using "banned" words or excessive hashtags (>2) will limit your reach to your immediate followers only.
- **Low-Quality Replying:** Replying with just emojis or "Nice!" to multiple accounts is flagged as "Bot-like" behavior.

## 🕒 Best Time to Post
𝕏 is the "Newsroom" of the internet. It moves faster than any other platform.
- **Weekdays:** 8:00 AM – 10:00 AM (Morning news catch-up).
- **The "Breaking" Window:** Any time between 1:00 PM and 3:00 PM.
- **Late Night:** 10:00 PM – 12:00 AM (High volume of tech and finance "night-owls").
- **Weekend Hack:** The "Saturday Deep Dive." Post a 10+ post thread on Saturday at 9:00 AM. Weekend users have a higher "Read-Through Rate" for long-form content than weekday "scrollers."

---

## 🛠️ Optimization Checklist
- **[ ] Post Frequency:** 3-5 times per day. 𝕏 is a high-volume platform; the "half-life" of a post is only about 18 minutes.
- **[ ] Grok Optimization:** Use relevant keywords in your bio and posts so that 𝕏’s AI (Grok) can categorize and recommend you.
- **[ ] Quote Posts:** Don't just Retweet; Quote Post (QP) with your own insight. The algorithm favors original commentary.
- **[ ] Communities:** Share your post into an 𝕏 Community for an immediate "base" of engagement.
