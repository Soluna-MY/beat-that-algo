# 🧶 Threads Algorithm Declassified

Threads is Meta’s "public square," and unlike Instagram, it is heavily skewed toward **conversational text**. The algorithm prioritizes high-quality discourse and "meaningful social interactions" over pure broadcast content.

## 🚀 Key Ranking Signals
1. **Replies (The Multiplier):** On Threads, a reply is more valuable than a "Like." If you spark a conversation, the algorithm boosts your post to the followers of everyone who replied.
2. **"For You" Feed Affinity:** Your post is shown to people who have interacted with similar topics, even if they don't follow you.
3. **Reposts:** Acts as a massive reach expander, pushing content into the "Following" feeds of others.
4. **Link Penalties:** External links (YouTube, articles) are often de-prioritized to keep users on the platform. **Pro Tip:** Post your link in the first reply, not the main post.

---

## ✍️ Writing for Maximum Reach
- **The "Thread" Structure:** Break long thoughts into a series of posts. Each sub-post in a thread is an opportunity for the algorithm to "grab" a different segment of the audience.
- **Micro-Hooks:** The first 80 characters must be a question or a controversial/intriguing statement.
- **No Engagement Bait:** Threads explicitly penalizes "Like this if..." or "Follow me for more..." tactics. Instead, use **Open-Loop Questions** (e.g., "What's the one thing everyone gets wrong about [Topic]?").
- **Voice & Tone:** Threads favors a "human" and slightly unpolished voice. Corporate-speak is the fastest way to get ignored.

## 📈 The "Hidden Reply" Growth Hack
One of the most effective ways to grow on Threads isn't posting—it's **Replying to Giants**.
1. Find 5-10 accounts in your niche with >50k followers.
2. Turn on post notifications for them.
3. Be one of the first 5 people to reply with a **high-value insight** (not just "Great post!").
4. Because the algorithm shows replies in the "For You" feed, their massive audience will see your profile.

## 🕒 Best Time to Post
Threads is currently most active during standard working hours as people look for "watercooler" conversation.
- **Weekdays:** 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM.
- **Evening Peak:** 8:00 PM – 10:00 PM (High conversational volume).
- **The "Mid-Week" Wave:** Wednesday and Thursday are consistently the highest engagement days.
- **Weekend Hack:** The "Saturday Morning Coffee" thread. Start a conversation early Saturday (8:00 AM) with a reflective or polarizing question. Users check Threads as their first app of the weekend; being the first voice in their feed ensures high reply volume.

---

## 🛠️ Optimization Checklist
- **[ ] Use ALT Text:** Threads is highly accessible; the algorithm favors posts with proper image descriptions.
- **[ ] Post Frequency:** 2-3 times per day is the current "sweet spot" for maintaining visibility without being flagged as spam.
- **[ ] Topic Tags:** Use the "Tag" feature (only one per post) to categorize your content for the Interest Graph.
- **[ ] Cross-Posting:** Avoid the "Share to Threads" button from Instagram for every post; native Threads content performs 4x better.
