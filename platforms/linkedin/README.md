# 👔 LinkedIn Algorithm Mastery

LinkedIn's algorithm is designed to prioritize **professional value** and **knowledge sharing**. Unlike other platforms, it heavily weights "Dwell Time" and the quality of the conversation over sheer volume.

## 🚀 Key Ranking Signals
1. **Dwell Time:** The amount of time users spend looking at your post. Long text posts and multi-page PDFs are currently the highest-performing formats because they force users to slow down.
2. **The "Golden Hour":** Engagement (Likes/Comments) received in the first 60 minutes after posting determines the "velocity" of the post.
3. **The "See More" Click:** When a user clicks "See More" to read your full post, it counts as a significant positive engagement signal.
4. **Relevant Comments:** The algorithm prioritizes comments that are more than 5 words long. Generic "Great post!" comments have minimal impact.

---

## 📄 The "Document" (PDF) Strategy
Currently, **PDF Carousels** are the most viral format on LinkedIn.
- **Why?** Each slide swipe counts as an engagement and increases Dwell Time.
- **Best Practice:** Keep it to 5-10 slides with high-contrast text and a clear headline on the first slide.
- **Export Tip:** Export your Canva or PowerPoint as a PDF and upload it as a "Document."

## ✍️ Content Engineering
- **The Hook:** The first 3 lines of your post are all a user sees before the "See More" button. This is your most valuable real estate.
- **Formatting:** Use whitespace! Avoid "walls of text." One idea per paragraph, max 2 lines per paragraph.
- **Tagging:** Only tag people if you are **100% sure** they will respond. If tagged users don't engage, the algorithm flags your post as "Spammy."
- **External Links:** LinkedIn wants to keep users on-site. **Never** put a link in the main post body. Instead, write "Link in the first comment" or add the link by editing the post 5 minutes after publishing.

## 📈 The "Social Selling Index" (SSI)
LinkedIn gives a "hidden" priority boost to accounts with a high SSI score.
- **Maintain your profile:** Ensure your "About" section and "Experience" are fully filled.
- **Consistent Outbound:** Engage with at least 3 posts in your feed before you hit "Post" on your own content.

## 🕒 Best Time to Post
LinkedIn is a "Workday" platform. Posting outside of 9-to-5 usually results in significantly lower reach.
- **Tuesday – Thursday:** 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM (The "Productive Window").
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings (people are catching up on emails) and Friday afternoons (people are mentally checked out).
- **The Weekend Rule:** Don't post on weekends unless it's a personal "reflection" post.
- **Weekend Hack:** The "Human Connection" post. Post a photo of yourself (no stock images) on Saturday at 10:00 AM with a lesson learned *outside* of work. These posts often receive 5x the engagement of professional posts because they stand out in a quiet feed.

---

## 🛠️ Optimization Checklist
- **[ ] Post Frequency:** 3-5 times per week. Quality > Quantity.
- **[ ] Hashtags:** Use exactly 3 hashtags. More than 5 is considered spam; fewer than 3 limits reach.
- **[ ] Personal Brand:** Use a "selfie" or personal photo once every 5 posts; human faces receive 3x more engagement on LinkedIn.
- **[ ] Replies:** Respond to every comment within the first 2 hours to keep the "velocity" high.
