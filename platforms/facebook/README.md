# 👥 Facebook Algorithm Decoded

Facebook's algorithm (the "News Feed" algorithm) is built on **Meaningful Social Interactions (MSI)**. It prioritizes content that sparks conversations between friends and family, but has recently shifted to compete with TikTok by heavily pushing **Reels** and **Public Group** content.

## 🚀 Key Ranking Signals
1. **Meaningful Social Interactions (MSI):** Long comments and replies between users are the #1 signal. A simple "Like" is the weakest signal.
2. **Relationship Score:** How often you interact with a specific person, page, or group. The more you interact, the higher their future content ranks in your feed.
3. **Content Type Affinity:** If a user watches 80% of videos but scrolls past photos, Facebook will prioritize video content for them.
4. **The "Reels" Bridge:** Reels are currently the only way to get massive reach outside of your existing followers on Facebook.

---

## 🏘️ The "Group First" Strategy
Groups are the heart of engagement on Facebook.
- **Engagement-First Posts:** Ask "Poll" questions. Polls are the highest-engagement tool in groups and signal to the algorithm that the group is "active."
- **Member-to-Member Value:** The algorithm favors posts that solve a problem for another member, triggering a long comment thread.
- **Link Posting:** Never post a link in a Group without a long (100+ word) original description. Facebook flags "naked links" as low-quality/spam.

## 🎞️ Facebook Reels: The New Growth Engine
Facebook is aggressively pushing Reels into the main feed.
- **Cross-Posting from Instagram:** Enable "Recommend on Facebook" in your Instagram settings. Facebook's Reels algorithm is less saturated than Instagram's, leading to 2x-5x higher reach for the same video.
- **On-Screen Captions:** 85% of Facebook users watch video with sound off. If you don't have captions, they will scroll past.

## ✍️ Writing for the "Family & Friends" Graph
- **The "Storytelling" Hook:** Facebook users prefer long-form storytelling. Start with a personal hook (e.g., "I never thought this would happen, but...") to trigger the MSI signal.
- **Avoid "Engagement Bait":** Facebook is very strict about "Share if you agree" or "Comment 'YES' below." These posts will be "shadow-demoted" (Reach limited to <1%).
- **Native Video:** Uploading a video directly to Facebook gets **10x more reach** than sharing a YouTube link.

## 🕒 Best Time to Post
Facebook engagement is highest during the "mid-day slump."
- **Monday – Friday:** 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM.
- **Peak Day:** Tuesday and Wednesday.
- **Groups:** Evenings (7:00 PM – 9:00 PM) are better for Group discussions when users are looking for community.
- **Weekend Hack:** Sunday "Family/Community" posts. Share a high-emotion story or a community event on Sunday at 2:00 PM. Facebook’s Family & Friends weight increases by 40% on weekends.

---

## 🛠️ Optimization Checklist
- **[ ] Post Frequency:** 1-2 times per day. Posting more than that often results in your second post cannibalizing the reach of the first.
- **[ ] Reply Speed:** Facebook rewards pages that respond to messages/comments within 15 minutes.
- **[ ] Share to Groups:** Share your Page's post into 2-3 **relevant** groups where you are an active member.
