# 🎵 TikTok Algorithm Decoded

TikTok’s algorithm is the gold standard of "Interest Graphs." It doesn't care who you follow; it cares about what you **watch**. To beat TikTok, you must master the "Initial Batch" and "Watch Time" metrics.

## 🚀 The "Batch Testing" System
TikTok doesn't show your video to everyone at once. It follows a tiered testing process:
1. **Tier 1 (The Initial Batch):** Your video is shown to 200–500 people.
2. **The Metric Check:** If the **Completion Rate** and **Watch Time** are high enough, it moves to Tier 2.
3. **Tier 2 (The Viral Surge):** Shown to 10k–50k people.
4. **The Final Push:** If engagement remains high, it hits the global "For You" page (FYP).

---

## 📈 Key Ranking Signals (Weighted)
1. **Average Watch Time / Completion Rate:** If users watch until the end or re-watch, TikTok pushes the video exponentially.
2. **Shares:** Signals that the content is "high-value" or "relatable."
3. **Saves:** Indicates "evergreen" value (e.g., a recipe or tutorial).
4. **SEO Keywords:** TikTok is now a search engine. Captions, on-screen text, and even the words you say are indexed.

## 🎞️ Content Engineering: The 1.5-Second Hook
TikTok users are "scrollers." If you don't stop them in 1.5 seconds, the video fails.
- **Visual Hook:** Sudden movement, text that "pops," or a dramatic change in lighting.
- **Psychological Hook:** "Stop scrolling if you want to [Benefit]" or "I finally found the secret to [Problem]."
- **Pacing:** Remove every "breath" or silence. TikTok content must be fast-paced and high-energy.

## 🔍 TikTok SEO Strategy
- **Keywords in Captions:** Use keywords that your target audience is searching for.
- **On-Screen Text:** The algorithm reads the text on your video. Use it to categorize your niche (e.g., "Budget Travel Tips").
- **Voiceover:** TikTok’s AI transcribes your audio. Explicitly saying your keywords out loud helps the algorithm find the right "Batch" to test your video on.

## 🕒 Best Time to Post
TikTok has global "hot spots" where the "Batch Testing" is most active.
- **Global Peaks:** Tuesday (9 AM), Thursday (12 PM), and Friday (5 AM).
- **Daily Strategy:** 7:00 PM – 11:00 PM (When the "scrolling" behavior is at its highest).
- **Consistency Note:** On TikTok, *when* you post is often less important than *how many* times you post per day.
- **Weekend Hack:** The "Saturday Binge" series. Post 3 related videos (Part 1, 2, and 3) within 2 hours on Saturday afternoon. If a user watches all three, TikTok identifies your content as "Binge-Worthy" and boosts your entire profile for the next 48 hours.

---

## 🛠️ Optimization Checklist
- **[ ] Post Frequency:** 1-3 times per day. Volume helps the algorithm categorize your account faster.
- **[ ] Trending Audio:** Use audio that is currently viral but lower the volume to 3% if you have a voiceover. This allows you to "ride the trend" without distracting from your message.
- **[ ] The "Comment Bridge":** Post a comment yourself as soon as you upload to start a conversation or ask a question.
- **[ ] Re-watch Loop:** Create a video that ends and begins seamlessly so the user accidentally watches it twice.
